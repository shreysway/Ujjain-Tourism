About Ujjain

Ujjain is an ancient city on the banks of the Kshipra River, rich with spiritual and cultural history. Known for the sacred Mahakaleshwar Temple, one of the twelve Jyotirlingas, Ujjain attracts devotees from all over. The city is celebrated for its temples, ashrams, and serene ghats, where visitors can experience deep spirituality and the essence of India’s cultural heritage.

Aside from the revered Mahakaleshwar, explore other landmarks like Ram Ghat, Harsiddhi Temple, and the tranquil banks of Shipra River, which comes alive during Kumbh Mela, a globally known spiritual gathering.
